About Us

Simul Technology Enthusiast Community (STEC), The word "Simul" is Latin meaning "Together" in the English Language; This means we are together is our passion for technology. STEC is a platform that caters to individuals that are enthusiastic about technology if you are a person that enjoys using, creating, buying, testing or learning new and existing technology; YOU ARE A TECH ENTHUSIAST.

Members of STEC do not need to have a background in Information Technology; diversity is one of the things we welcome. The foundation of STEC is the interest in all things technology big or small, which is why we ask that all members should be technology lovers.

We are here to grow a community out of our shared interest in technology where individuals can network, learn, collaborate and build with like-minded people with diverse backgrounds.
